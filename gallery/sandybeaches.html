Diani Beach

Kenya's most famous beach with 17km of pristine white sand, coral reefs, and excellent water sports facilities.

Best Time: December to March (dry season)
All year: Water activities available year-round

Malindi Beach

Historic coastal town with beautiful beaches, nearby marine park, and excellent snorkeling and diving opportunities.

Best Time: October to April (calm seas)
Year-round: Cultural attractions always open
Book Now

Watamu Beach

Protected marine park with coral gardens, sea turtles, and some of the best diving spots in East Africa.

Best Time: October to March (best visibility)
All year: Marine park accessible year-round
Book Now

Nyali Beach

Popular beach in Mombasa with luxury resorts, water sports, and easy access to city attractions.

Best Time: December to March (ideal weather)
Year-round: Resort facilities always available

Lamu Archipelago

UNESCO World Heritage site with pristine beaches, Swahili culture, and traditional dhow sailing experiences.

Best Time: December to March (festival season)
All year: Cultural experiences year-round
Book Now

Tiwi Beach

Quiet, unspoiled beach perfect for relaxation, with tidal pools and excellent swimming conditions.
